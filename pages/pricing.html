Pricing Intelligence FAQ

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your engagement level at any time. Engaged resources will be reallocated within 24 hours of a tier change notification.

Are there any hidden deploy fees?

Our pricing is transparent. Foundation tiers use community clusters, while SaaS and Enterprise tiers include dedicated infrastructure overhead in the monthly commitment.

What defines a 'Lead Engineer'?

Enterprise partners are assigned a dedicated senior architect with 10+ years of domain experience to oversee system integrity, security audits, and deployment strategy.

Do you offer custom SLA targets?

Absolutely. Enterprise tiers support custom Service Level Agreements (SLA) with guaranteed response times and high-availability (99.99%) uptime commitments.
